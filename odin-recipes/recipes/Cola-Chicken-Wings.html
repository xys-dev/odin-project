Cola Chicken Wings

Description

Cola Chicken Wings is a popular dish that combines the savory flavor of chicken wings with the sweet and fizzy taste of cola. The result is a deliciously sticky and flavorful dish that is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings ~500g
  • Cola ~1 can (330ml)
  • Soy Sauce ~2 tbsp
  • Garlic ~3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger ~3 slices
  • Green Onions ~2 stalks, chopped
  • Salt ~to taste
  • Pepper ~to taste
